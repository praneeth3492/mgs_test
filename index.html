Frequently Asked Questions

Does your school provide transport?

MGS provides a school bus facility covering nearly all parts of Hyderabad, complete with designated pick-up and drop-off points. Our own fleet of air-conditioned buses is equipped with GPS, and each bus has a female safety warden, usually accompanied by a teacher in charge

Does your school provide meals?

For day scholars, we provide breakfast and lunch, as well as two snacks, while for boarders, we provide all meals

What are the co-curricular and sports facilities available?

The 20-acre green campus has everything needed to support an internationally designed learning and leadership curriculum. To promote overall growth, the school property features smart classrooms, activity rooms, a fitness centre, art and music studios, libraries, large playing and sporting fields, extensive gardens, playgrounds, and a swimming pool. Dedicated coaches in all major sports are trained with the most up-to-date training equipment and methodologies.

What is the admission assessment for?

The assessment is not a criterion for admission, but it is to help us understand and create the child portfolio.

Where Is the school?

Our campus is located on the Srisailam Highway and covers 20-acre lush green land. It is a 20-minute drive from the International Airport Shamshabad.

Do you offer boarding facilities?

Yes, we have separate boarding facilities for boys and girls, offering a nurturing space where students can concentrate on their studies and personal growth. Our unique 5-day boarding policy provides students with a balanced home-school life.

How is technology integrated into learning?

We expand learning opportunities by utilising cutting-edge technology and innovative teaching strategies, thereby preparing students for a tech-driven world.

What facilities does the school provide?

Our state-of-the-art infrastructure includes modern classrooms, laboratories, an amphitheatre, a library, and even a Sky Observatory and a Planetarium, enriching the learning experience.
