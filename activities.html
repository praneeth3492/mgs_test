Home to Limitless Creativity and Exciting Extracurricular Exploration

Manchester Global School is excited to introduce a wide range of exciting extracurricular activities that will inspire our students' passion and self-discovery. Your child will enter a world of limitless opportunities, where their skills will be developed, their imaginations will fly, and their spirits will soar.

Imagine your child performing in our lovely outdoor amphitheatre while standing under the open sky, their voice resonating in the space. Here, kids can let their dramatic talents run wild while engrossing themselves in the allure of theatre which can accommodate more than 150 people. As they embrace the spotlight, building confidence and expressing their unique voice, their eyes will shine with delight.

The energetic beats in our vivacious dance space will reverberate with the excitement and energy of young dancers. Your child will dance joyfully as they experiment with many dance genres, from classical to contemporary. Their movements will communicate tales that words cannot, and their bodies will serve as a canvas for self-expression.

Through their time in the music rooms, your child will learn to play instruments, unlocking the secrets of notes and chords that will transport them to new realms of expression. Our passionate music teachers will guide your child on a transformative journey.

The atmosphere in our sound recording studio will be electric as aspiring young artists put their ideas to life. The chance for your youngster to learn about the field of sound engineering, practice with recording methods, and create their own song. Their inventiveness and creativity would be evident in every rhythm and note.

Your child will enjoy learning about the benefits of physical exercise and well-being in our well-equipped gym. Children will engage in energising workouts under the direction of qualified trainers, gain strength, and form lifelong healthy habits.

Our Yoga Room is specially designed to let your child find comfort in the fusion of mind, body, and spirit. The little ones will pick up age-old mindfulness, breathing, and relaxation practices, giving them the skills they need to face life's obstacles with grace and resiliency.




At Manchester Global School, we believe that extracurricular activities are more than just pastimes, they are also doors to self-knowledge and development. Through these events, kids develop a strong sense of community as they join together, help one another, and make connections that last a lifetime.
